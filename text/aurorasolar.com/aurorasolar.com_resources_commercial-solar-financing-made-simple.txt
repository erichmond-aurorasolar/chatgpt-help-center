The commercial solar financing process has been notoriously lengthy and complicated. A large part of this is due to inefficiencies in the application and due diligence processes that financiers require to fund projects. In this webinar, we’re joined by our new partners at Sustainable Capital Finance (SCF) who will show you how installers can get access to commercial financing faster and more easily.
 
In this webinar, you’ll:

See how our new partnership will enable you to get financing more easily
Learn how live financing rates can help you land more customers
See how Vivint Solar optimized their commercial design and sales process
