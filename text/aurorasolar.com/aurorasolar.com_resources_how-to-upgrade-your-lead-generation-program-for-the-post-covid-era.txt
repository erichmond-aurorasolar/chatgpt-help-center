In the post-Covid era, solar installers will be increasingly in need of lead-generation that doesn’t rely on door-to-door knocking. In this webinar, we’re joined by the CEO of Zenernet, who will show you how to diversify your lead-gen efforts to set yourself up for long-term success. We’ll discuss how to set up a referral program, how to bring in leads through digital marketing, and how to leverage your local brand online to give you an edge over the bigger solar installers in the industry.
You’ll learn:

How to create a more effective referral program
How to use digital marketing to increase your lead sources
How to differentiate yourself from bigger solar installers
