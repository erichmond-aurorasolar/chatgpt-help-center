Designing a high-performing PV system is win-win for installers and customers alike, but getting absolute maximum power out of a system can be tricky. In this webinar, we’ll show you how Aurora’s latest remote system design and performance simulation tools that can make designing high-performing systems easy. We’ll also demonstrate 3 techniques that you can use to optimize the solar energy yield of your systems.
In this webinar, you’ll:

See how shading impacts system performance and how to mitigate shade losses
Learn how to design systems that optimize for Time of Use rates
Find out when it may make sense to increase the DC-to-AC ratio of a design
