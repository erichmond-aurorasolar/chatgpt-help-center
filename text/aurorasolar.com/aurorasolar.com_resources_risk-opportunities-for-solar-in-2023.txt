After huge economic/policy changes in 2022 and with more looming ahead, it’s more important than ever for solar companies to stay agile and adaptable. In this webinar, we’ll discuss what the biggest risks and opportunities are for the industry this year and strategies you can use to navigate them.
We’ll be joined by speakers from Aurora Solar, Renu Energy, and Sungage, who will share some of their best strategies and predictions for 2023. 
You will learn how to:
Take advantage of the opportunities IRA brings.Navigate the challenges of NEM and supply chain issues.Leverage financing options in a rising rate environment.
