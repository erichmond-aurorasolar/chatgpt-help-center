According to our recent survey, over 70% of installers are already offering storage as a way to bring in much needed revenue. In this webinar, we’re going to take a deep dive into our market research and show you how you can position storage and model its benefits to close more sales. We’ll also be joined by Generac who will share how they recommend positing storage to potential buyers. Plus, we’ll do a live demo of how to model storage and show some examples of successful sales proposals
You’ll learn:

How to position storage to increase your solar + storage sales
What tools you can use to model storage for a potential customer
How to overcome the common objections to storage
