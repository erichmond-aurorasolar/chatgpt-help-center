When four solar companies joined forces to form Lumio, it created some unique challenges. Chief among them was aligning all the new company’s disparate teams on one solar design software, and onboarding them on the software in the best way possible. In this session, Paul Tollefson shares why Lumio chose Aurora to be that software. They’ll go in-depth on how they aligned pre-sale design with sales, and even post-sale, and how Aurora helps them achieve their goals, including faster turnaround times while maintaining design quality.
