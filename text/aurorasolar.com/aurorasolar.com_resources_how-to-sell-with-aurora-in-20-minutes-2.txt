“The early bird gets the worm,” or in this case the solar sale. One of the best ways you can give yourself a competitive advantage is by providing a homeowner with a quick and visually appealing solar proposal.
In this bite-sized webinar we’ll show you how to go from meeting a customer to a complete sales proposal in less than 20 minutes. We’ll take you step-by-step through finding the optimally sized system, placing panels on the customer’s roof, and even providing them with financing options.
You’ll learn how to:
Create a site design in less than 30 secondsDetermine consumption with a partial utility billOptimally place panels on the customer’s roofProvide multiple financing options to close the deal
