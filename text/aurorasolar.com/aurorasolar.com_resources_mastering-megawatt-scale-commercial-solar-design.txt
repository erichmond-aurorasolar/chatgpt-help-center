Large commercial solar projects can be excellent business opportunities for solar contractors, but the difference between profit and loss is having an efficient design process and the right tools. In this webinar, we will highlight design strategies for commercial megawatt-scale solar projects, including how to accurately account for shading, optimize stringing configurations, check for NEC violations, and maximize the energy output from rooftop and carport projects.
In this webinar, you’ll:

Learn how to automatically optimize stringing for lower costs and higher production
See time-saving tools like carport templates, automatic stringing, streamlined walkway placement, and “fill zones”
Find out how to reduce change orders with tools like NEC validation, automated obstruction detection, and LIDAR
