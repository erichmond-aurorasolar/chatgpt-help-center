Most solar installers might be leaving a lot of money on the table by only installing solar panels. As the economy slows down, it’s more important than ever that solar installers get the maximum value out of every single project.
In this webinar, we’ll share the best way installers can add new sources of revenue to their pipeline including storage, EV charging, energy efficiency, and monitoring systems. We’ll also cover how to improve long term revenue through better post-sale customer service.
Attendees of this webinar will learn:
How to properly pitch additional products to prospects without scaring them away.How to create a complete sales proposal that is easy to understand.How to continue to generate revenue even after the sale is complete.
