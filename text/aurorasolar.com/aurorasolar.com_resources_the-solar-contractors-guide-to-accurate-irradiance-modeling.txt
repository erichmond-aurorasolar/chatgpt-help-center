Solar irradiance, or the amount of available solar energy, at a particular location is one of the most fundamental pieces of information when designing a solar PV system. Solar software makes it easy to calculate irradiance with the click of a button. In this webinar, we’ll take a peek behind the curtain and look at how irradiance is calculated behind the scenes. This will help you better answer customer questions and ensure you’re using the best tools to provide accurate irradiance and production estimates.
In this webinar, you’ll:

Learn how irradiance is calculated and the different components of irradiance
Discover new software features for improved irradiance values
Find out how Aurora gets 30X improvements in the speed of its irradiance calculations
