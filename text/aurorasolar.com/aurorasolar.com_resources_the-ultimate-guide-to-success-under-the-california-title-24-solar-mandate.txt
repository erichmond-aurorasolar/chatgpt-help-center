In 2018, the California Energy Commission (CEC) updated the state’s Building Energy Efficiency Standards, also knowns as Title 24. The update requires that construction of virtually all new homes permitted on or after January 1, 2020 must include advanced energy efficiency.

We created this guide to help you navigate the new Title 24 changes and to provide step-by-step instructions for how to accurately design Title 24-approved solar PV systems on new homes using Aurora.

Inside you’ll find:
– An overview of CA’s new Building Energy Efficiency Standard
– Everything you need to know about the new solar mandate
– Step-by-step instructions on how to use Aurora to ensure compliance

Download the guide and start learning everything you need to know about Title 24.
