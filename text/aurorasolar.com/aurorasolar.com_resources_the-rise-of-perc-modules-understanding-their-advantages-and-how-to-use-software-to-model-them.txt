Over the last few years PERC solar panels have become increasingly popular. In this webinar, we will be joined by LONGi—a major mono-silicon solar panel manufacturer, producing one of the most efficient PERC panels. We will cover the basics of PERC technology, its benefits, and how to confidently design a solar project using PERC modules.
In this webinar, you’ll:

Learn about the basics of PERC solar cell technology
Understand what to look for when shopping for PERC modules
See how to design and sell with PERC panels remotely
