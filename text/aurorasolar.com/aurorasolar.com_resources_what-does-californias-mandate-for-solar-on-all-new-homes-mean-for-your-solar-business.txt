California made history on May 9th, 2018, when it became the first state to mandate solar PV systems on all new homes. According to the CALSSA, the number of new homes built with solar in California will quadruple as a result. By making solar a new normal for homebuyers, the policy will also bring solar energy further into the mainstream. In this webinar, we will share how solar contractors can adapt their design processes to take advantage of new construction.
In this webinar, you’ll:

Learn the key requirements of California’s new solar mandate
Find out what new business opportunities small and medium-sized solar contractors will see
Discover how you can design a solar system using just a roof plan and Aurora
