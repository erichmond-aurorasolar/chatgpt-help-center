Much of the conversations and headlines surrounding SB 100 starts and ends with the three renewable portfolio standard targets—50% renewables by 2026, 60% renewables by 2030, and 100% clean energy by 2045. But what does California’s goal of reaching 100% clean energy mean for distributed solar and the industry?
In this webinar, we sit down with Bernadette Del Chiaro, Executive Director of the CALSSA and Ken Rider from the California Energy Commission to answer your questions and discuss SB 100.
You’ll learn:

What is SB 100 and how will it affect solar installers
What will California’s new goals mean for distributed solar
What do top policy makers expect to happen in the next few years
