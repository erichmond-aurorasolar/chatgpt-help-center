Aurora Solar pioneered the use of LIDAR data, which provides a 3D map of an area to calculate building dimensions and tree heights, allowing solar contractors to avoid traveling to a site before making a final quote. Today, we’re excited to announce our partnership with Google, which will make your solar sales and design processes even more accurate.
In this webinar, you’ll learn:

How our new partnership with Google will improve your 3D data
How to reduce on site visits (truck rolls) using software
How much the cost of a solar installation can be reduced using 3D data
