Once you’ve landed a lead and successfully pitched them on solar, there is usually one last hurdle to overcome: convincing them to sign that contract. In the past, this process has been riddled with problems that led to change orders and, worst of all, cancellations.
But now Aurora’s new Sales Mode with Contract Manager can virtually eliminate this last hurdle. Join us for this micro-webinar to see how to smooth out the “final mile” of your sales process so you can reduce cancellations and increase close rates.
You’ll learn:
How to adapt your proposal in real-time based on customer feedbackHow to increase your close rates by improving your customer’s experienceHow to get the customer’s approval faster with Contract Manager
