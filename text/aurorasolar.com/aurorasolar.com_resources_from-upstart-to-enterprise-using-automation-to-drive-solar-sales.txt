Inefficient processes cost the average solar project an extra $7,000. Permitting, change orders, and clunky workflows add significant extra costs to a solar project and can drag your solar business down.
In this webinar, we’ll be joined by Infinity Energy, one of the top solar installers in the solar industry who have achieved 2-3x the average growth rate of their industry peer group. By streamlining critical steps in their process, they’ve been able to cut their design time from 1.5 hours to just 20 minutes.
We’ll examine the automations and workflows that have enabled them to scale and hear how solar software has been a key driver in enabling their success.
You’ll learn: 

How to speed up your lead to sale time
How to make your design workflows more efficient
How to use AI-driven solar software to reduce soft costs
