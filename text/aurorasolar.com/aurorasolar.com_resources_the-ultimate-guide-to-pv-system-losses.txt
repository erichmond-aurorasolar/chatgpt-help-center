This guide highlights the leading causes of energy losses in solar PV systems, and how you can avoid them. You’ll get tips for improving the accuracy of your performance simulations as well as research-backed recommendations for different loss types.
Inside you’ll learn:
How basics such as tilt, orientation, and shade affect system performanceHow equipment mismatches can cause hidden inefficienciesAdvanced concepts, including incident angle modifiers, module nameplate rating losses, and more.
