With the adoption of CA’s new mandate, Title 24, most new residential construction in California will now require solar. These changes will affect the homebuilding industry in many ways, including design plans, project workflow, required permitting documents, and tools needed to demonstrate compliance. In this webinar, we are joined by the California Energy Commission and a seasoned energy consultant to share the key compliance requirements and what changes you should know about.
In this webinar, you’ll:
– Get a quick overview of the Solar Mandate– Learn about key compliance requirements (and exceptions)– Find out how to use software to make compliance easier
