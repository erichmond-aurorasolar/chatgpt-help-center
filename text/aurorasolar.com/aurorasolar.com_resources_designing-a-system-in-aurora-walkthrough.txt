This Aurora workshop walks you through everything you need to know about designing a system in Aurora. We’ll cover preliminary design settings, the best design features available in Aurora, and best practices for your post-design process. 
This A-Z design workshop covers:
How to enter the setback requirements into the interfaceHow and when to use LIDARHow to generate bankable shade reportsPlan Sets, DXF files, and more
