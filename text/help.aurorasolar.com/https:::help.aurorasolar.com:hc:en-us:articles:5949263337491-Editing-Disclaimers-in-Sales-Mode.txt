Editing Disclaimers in Sales Mode

Each page in Sales Mode has a subtext section at the bottom, where you may enter in disclaimers, warranty information, or any other information you wish to display to your prospects. Admins can manage their configurations from within the app.
 


As an admin user, navigate to Sales Mode Customization settings
Find the page you'd like to edit, then click Edit (as shown in the above image)
Scroll down to the Disclaimers section
Edit the disclaimers section and click Done
