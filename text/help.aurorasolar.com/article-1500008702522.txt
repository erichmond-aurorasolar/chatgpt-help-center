What is Aurora’s Enterprise Solution? 
Aurora's Enterprise solution is designed for companies looking to grow and scale their business. Each Aurora Enterprise implementation is customized based on our customers’ needs. 
 
Enterprise features include:  

Everything in Premium, plus:
API integration with your CRM and the rest of your tech stack
Secure Mode permission setting
Proposal template design service
Monthly analytics reports
3D modeling service (discounted pricing)
Account configuration
Dedicated customer success manager
Custom team trainings
