Map Imagery Selection

Overview
Selecting the map imagery area within a design is possible through the map imagery selection modal which allows:

Faster and easier map imagery resizing for standard maps (Google Maps, Google Maps HD, and Bing Maps)
The ability to both increase and reduce the size of the map image 

How to access the modal
There are 2 ways to access the feature, using the map arrows or from the Map selection menu.
1. Map arrows
Click on the arrows to access the modal where you can resize the map.

2. Resize map button next to the Maps menu
Click on the resize button to access the modal where you can resize the map.

Resizing your map
Map resizing modal
This is the map resizing modal. Resize the box to the desired map image size and click on update maps to save and view changes.
