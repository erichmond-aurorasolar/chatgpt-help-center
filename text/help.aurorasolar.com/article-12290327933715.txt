Creating a 3D Site Model in New Aurora

There are 3 ways that you can create a site model within Aurora and the method that you choose will be determined by your account plan and your company's desired workflow.

 
Within Sales Mode, you'll want to create your site model either using Aurora AI or with a design that has already been created manually -- either by our Expert Design Services team or one that you've drawn yourself using our Smart Roof tool in Design Mode.
Follow the links below to learn more about each method of generating a site model in Aurora.
