9/21/18 Release Notes


Added ability to select weather data sets for PVWatts simulation. The options are Default, NSRDB-PSM3, TMY3, TMY2, International and can be found in the performance simulation settings.
Expanded project search capabilities. Users can now do partial searches of projects names.
Revised incentive tax wording. The revision was done on the summary of the tax section in Financing. 
Improved the Mppt assignment Algorithm to minimize crashing. 
Added ability to handle large image files when uploading custom imagery. Aurora will automatically resize files that are larger than 4096x4096.
