Solar-Style Loan (Overview)




A solar-style loan is a popular loan type for financing the cost of a solar installation. Solar financing companies such as Mosaic and Sunlight all offer various loan packages. Here is how to model it:

Go to your account Database. Click on Financing Products, then Add Financing Product. Give your template a descriptive name, then click Create.

On the next page, click Add Loan.

Give your loan a descriptive name that you will later be able to understand.

Enter Principal percentage.Principal percentage should be: 100% - Down Payment%. So if the homeowner is putting no money down, this should be 100%.

Input your 'Dealer Fee', 'Interest Rate', and the 'Duration' of the loan.


If a prepayment will be made, check the box.

Enter the expected prepayment amount and expected prepayment month.

You may choose to lock your changes, so other users cannot change the parameters you have chosen.

Click OK.
