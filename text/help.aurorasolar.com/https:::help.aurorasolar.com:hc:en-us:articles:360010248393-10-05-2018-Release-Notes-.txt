10/05/2018 Release Notes 


Modified and speed up fill zone labels - labels are now always visible. They will automatically fade out if the label collides with other labels. Edge labels are now always visible in ortho view.

Improved proposal text box usability - Left clicking on the text of a text box will allow you to edit the text. User can now drag the text box by clicking on a location that has no text.

Added a new commercial template called “Aurora - Commercial Template 2”
