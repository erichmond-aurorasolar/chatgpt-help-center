How to Change the Font in a Proposal

         
Aurora gives you the ability to change the type, size, and color of your font.

At the top right corner, click on your Name then on Settings in the drop down menu.
On the left menu, click on Proposal Template and select the template you would like to adjust the font in.

Right-click on the text box. In the pop-up box, choose the type of font from the drop-down menu. You can also change the font size. For the color, you will need to click on show more.
