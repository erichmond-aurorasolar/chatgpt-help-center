Validating Designs


On the Design page, click the page icon located in the top toolbar. 

Click on the + symbol to expand each category.
Click on the name of the validation test to display the mathematical equations used.

        

An electrical tree diagram will display the electrical connections of your system.
Aurora will display validation warnings and/or errors based on your design.


To find and correct/adjust the specific point of error with your layout, simply click on the name of the component. Click on the blue return arrow to view the component in the Database.
