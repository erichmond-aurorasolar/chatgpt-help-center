How to cancel an account

To cancel your account entirely, please email our team at ar@aurorasolar.com. 
Please note that account cancellations are final. All Designs should be exported if you wish to retain them. Please see section 9.3 of our Terms of Use for more information. 
If you would like to change a user type or remove a user's license, please read the steps in the article How to Add, Edit or Remove a User License (Administrators Only).
