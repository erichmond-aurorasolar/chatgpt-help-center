Data Sources used in Aurora for Shading Analysis

Aurora uses a variety of data sources to run shading and performance analyses. They are the following:
Weather Data sources: 

National Renewable Energy Laboratory - TMY3, PSM V3
Canadian  Engineering Climate Services Unit - CWEC
ASHRAE - International Weather for Energy Calculations, v 2.0

Horizon Shading Data is based on data from Mapzen and OpenStreetMap. Aurora uses the elevation data to create a horizon profile for shade analysis: 

Mapzen
Openstreetmap
Others Resources
