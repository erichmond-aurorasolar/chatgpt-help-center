Click on the Connect icon, then starting from either string end, click to connect your string(s) to your inverter/combiner box.

Think of connections like conduit runs on the roof/wall
Continue to click on components to connect them together

         
For microinverter systems, the 'string' feature connects modules in parallel on a single trunk cable. Red indicates the trunk cable ampacity has been exceeded, where the trunk cable ampacity is defaulted to the standard 20 amps.
