AutoDesigner: Savings Mode


Within your Design, click on AutoDesigner in the toolbar (it's located in the top right).

Select the mode: Savings

Input a dollar per month savings target and set the post-solar rate.
Select your components (by clicking on Select).
Select the panel orientation and the price basis (optional).
Click on Run.
You can change the post-solar rate by clicking on Change.You can change the selected components by clicking on Edit.
