12/10/2020 Release Notes

Features Added

Added an UPDATE endpoint for setting incentives on the financing tab.

Features Updated

Fixed an issue that caused cash grants to not be included in the correct column on the financial analysis spreadsheet.
Fixed an issue that caused the year to apply for a financial incentive to not be last year of the project life cycle.
Fixed an issue that caused financial incentives to show on financing graphs as the year after they are actually applied. 
Updated tooltips around financing metrics and incentives.
Fixed an issue related to storage system size incentives that include a hybrid inverter. 
Fixed an issue with SE Prism inverters causing the incorrect DC to AC ratio to populate by default.
Fixed an issue that caused the simulate button on the financing page to not reset properly after the simulation had been completed.
Introduced new and updated utility rates to the consumption database
