How to Design a Ground Mount System

Follow these steps to create a ground mount system:

In the Design section, click System, Insert Panels and select Groundmounts/Carports.  
Select the module type and panel orientation. You can enter a tilt value, height to base, add strings of length, and add module spacing in the advanced section below. Once you've made your selections click Draw.


Left-click where you would like the top left of the array to start. Move your cursor to the right until you have the desired number of columns then left-click again.


Move your cursor down to determine the number of rows then left-click.Once you’ve placed the ground mount you can make adjustments to the rows, columns, and spacing using the fields in the right sidebar.Click here to learn how to create Carport Templates.
