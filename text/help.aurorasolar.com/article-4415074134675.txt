How to download the Proposal Summary in Sales Mode


Once you’ve completed your design, pricing, and financing, – the print proposal consolidates all the information from your proposal into a print ready format. To access this, click on this dropdown menu within Sales Mode, and go down to the Share button.

This will give you 2 options – first you could send the homeowner a link to an interactive Web Proposal, or you can click Download to view your proposal details in a PDF format.
