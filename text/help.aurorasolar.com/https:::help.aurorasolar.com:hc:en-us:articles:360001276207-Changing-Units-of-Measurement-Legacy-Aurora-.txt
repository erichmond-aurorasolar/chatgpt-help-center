Changing Units of Measurement (Legacy Aurora)

To change the unit of metric measurement associated with your account and projects, please follow these easy steps:
 

Navigate to your name listed in the top-right hand corner of Aurora. 
Next click on 'Settings' in the drop-down menu. 
Then in the left-hand toolbar, click on 'General' 
Lastly click on 'Units' and change units from 'Imperial' to 'Metric'
Lastly change the unit of temperature from C to F as needed.
